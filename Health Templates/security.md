## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in {{project}}, please **do not** open
a public issue. Instead, email {{email}} with details.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and aim to provide a fix or
mitigation timeline within 7 days. Thank you for helping keep the project and
its users safe.
